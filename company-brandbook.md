## 페이지 2

01 Brand Overview
"For the Safest Black
in the Universe."
Melanoir is a deep-tech bio-material
company that develops high-
performance melanin-based
solutions for beauty, medical, and
industrial applications. They
specialize in engineering pure
melanin to create the 'safest black'
for products ranging from tattoo
and PMU inks to advanced
cosmetic photoprotection and
radiation shielding.
